Diet & Nutrition

A balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains is crucial for brain health. Consider the MIND diet, which combines aspects of the Mediterranean and DASH diets to create a meal plan focused on brain health.

Physical Activity
expand_more

Regular physical activity increases blood flow to the brain and can help maintain brain function. Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity exercise per week, such as brisk walking or swimming.

Social Engagement
expand_more

Staying socially active can support brain health. Engage in activities you enjoy that involve other people, such as joining a club, volunteering, or spending regular time with friends and family.

Mental Stimulation
expand_more

Challenge your mind regularly. Learn a new skill, play strategy games, do puzzles, or read challenging books. Keeping your brain active may help build reserves of brain cells and connections.
